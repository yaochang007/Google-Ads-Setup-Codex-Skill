# Google Ads Search Campaign Planning Checklist

## Inputs

- [ ] Business goal and offer are clear.
- [ ] Conversion goal is identified.
- [ ] Landing pages are supplied.
- [ ] Locations and languages are supplied.
- [ ] Budget cap or budget range is supplied.
- [ ] Bidding proposal is documented.
- [ ] Keyword themes and negatives are documented.
- [ ] Compliance and brand constraints are documented.

## Safety

- [ ] No browser automation was run.
- [ ] No Google Ads account was opened.
- [ ] No campaign was created, saved, published, launched, enabled, or paused.
- [ ] Budget and bidding remain proposals only.

## Approval

- [ ] Human approved the plan for draft setup with
      `approve draft <campaign-name>`, or requested changes.
