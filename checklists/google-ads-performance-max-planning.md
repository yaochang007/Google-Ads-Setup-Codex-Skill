# Google Ads Performance Max Planning Checklist

## Inputs

- [ ] Business objective and primary conversion goal are clear.
- [ ] Conversion tracking readiness is confirmed or blocked.
- [ ] Budget cap and bidding objective are documented.
- [ ] Asset groups and assets are complete enough for draft setup.
- [ ] Audience signals are documented.
- [ ] Final URL expansion and URL exclusions are documented.
- [ ] Brand exclusions or brand safety constraints are documented.
- [ ] Merchant Center/feed readiness is documented if ecommerce applies.

## Safety

- [ ] No browser automation was run.
- [ ] No Google Ads account was opened.
- [ ] No campaign was created, saved, published, launched, enabled, or paused.
- [ ] Budget, bidding, audience signals, and final URL expansion remain
      proposals only.

## Approval

- [ ] Human approved the plan for draft setup with
      `approve draft <campaign-name>`, or requested changes.
