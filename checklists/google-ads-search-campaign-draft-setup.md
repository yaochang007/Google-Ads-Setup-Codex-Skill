# Google Ads Search Campaign Draft Setup Checklist

## Preflight

- [ ] Account name and customer ID were confirmed.
- [ ] Approved Search campaign plan was available.
- [ ] Gate B approval was present.
- [ ] Budget, bidding, locations, languages, landing pages, conversion goal,
      keywords, negatives, and ads matched the approved plan.

## Safety

- [ ] Campaign setup stopped before final save.
- [ ] Campaign setup stopped before publish, enable, or launch.
- [ ] No billing, account access, conversion action, recommendation, enabled
      campaign budget, or enabled campaign bidding change was made.
- [ ] Any unexpected modal or mismatch caused a stop.

## Handoff

- [ ] Draft summary was produced.
- [ ] Final launch review was identified as the next step.
