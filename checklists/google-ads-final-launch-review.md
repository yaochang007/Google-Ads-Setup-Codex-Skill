# Google Ads Final Launch Review Checklist

## Verification

- [ ] Account name and customer ID were confirmed.
- [ ] Draft campaign name and type were confirmed.
- [ ] Campaign status was verified.
- [ ] Budget exactly matched the expected value.
- [ ] Bidding strategy and targets matched expectations.
- [ ] Conversion goals and tracking readiness were verified.
- [ ] Locations and languages matched expectations.
- [ ] Network/final URL settings matched expectations.
- [ ] Ads, assets, asset groups, ad groups, keywords, and negatives were
      reviewed as applicable.
- [ ] Landing pages and tracking were reviewed.
- [ ] Billing readiness was verified from a recent account audit.

## Approval

- [ ] Gate C QA sign-off is complete.
- [ ] Gate D approval is present only if launch execution is requested.
- [ ] Gate D approval exactly names the campaign and budget:
      `approve publish <campaign-name> budget <number>`.

## Safety

- [ ] No publish, enable, launch, final save, pause, remove, budget, bidding,
      conversion, billing, account access, recommendation, targeting, asset, ad,
      keyword, negative, tracking, or Merchant Center change happened without
      the specific required approval.
- [ ] The skill stopped on any mismatch or uncertainty.
