# Google Ads Budget and Bidding Review Checklist

## Review

- [ ] Account identity and date range were confirmed.
- [ ] Campaign budget state was reviewed in read-only mode.
- [ ] Bidding strategies and targets were reviewed in read-only mode.
- [ ] CPA/ROAS or the relevant KPI was reviewed where available.
- [ ] Google budget/bidding recommendations were read but not applied.
- [ ] All recommendations are labeled proposal-only.

## Safety

- [ ] No budget was changed.
- [ ] No bidding strategy or target was changed.
- [ ] No recommendation was applied.
- [ ] No campaign was enabled, paused, launched, published, or saved.
- [ ] No billing, account access, conversion action, targeting, or tracking
      change was made.

## Future Approval

- [ ] Any future budget change is mapped to Gate E.
- [ ] Any future bidding change is mapped to Gate F.
