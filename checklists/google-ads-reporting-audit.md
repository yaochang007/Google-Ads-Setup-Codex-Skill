# Google Ads Reporting Audit Checklist

## Read-Only Coverage

- [ ] Account identity and date range were confirmed.
- [ ] Account health was reviewed in read-only mode.
- [ ] Campaign performance was reviewed in read-only mode.
- [ ] Conversion reporting was reviewed in read-only mode.
- [ ] Spend, CPA, and ROAS were reviewed where available.
- [ ] Search terms were reviewed without adding negatives.
- [ ] Assets and ads were reviewed without editing.
- [ ] Recommendations were reviewed without applying or dismissing.

## Safety Confirmation

- [ ] No campaigns, ads, assets, reports, recommendations, budgets, bidding, or
      conversion actions were changed.
- [ ] No save, apply, publish, enable, pause, continue, or confirm action was
      clicked.

## Gate A

- [ ] Human reviewed the report.
- [ ] Human approved with `approve audit` or `audit looks good`, or requested
      corrections.
