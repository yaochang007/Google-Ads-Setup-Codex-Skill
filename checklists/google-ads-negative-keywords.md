# Google Ads Negative Keywords Checklist

## Review

- [ ] Account and customer ID were confirmed.
- [ ] Search term date range and scope were confirmed.
- [ ] Proposal separates observed search terms from proposed negatives.
- [ ] Each negative has match type, scope, reason, and risk.
- [ ] Valuable or ambiguous terms were kept out of the negative list.

## Safety

- [ ] No negative keyword was added without exact approval.
- [ ] No negative keyword was removed.
- [ ] No budgets, bidding, status, recommendations, billing, account access, or
      conversion actions were changed.
- [ ] Any save/apply step showed only the approved negative keyword changes.

## Approval

- [ ] Human approved exact scope and count with
      `approve negatives <scope>: <count> terms`, or requested changes.
