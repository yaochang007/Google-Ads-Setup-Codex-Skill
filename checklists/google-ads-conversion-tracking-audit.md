# Google Ads Conversion Tracking Audit Checklist

## Read-Only Coverage

- [ ] Account name and customer ID were confirmed.
- [ ] User role/access level was recorded if visible.
- [ ] Conversion goals were inspected in read-only mode.
- [ ] Conversion actions were inspected in read-only mode.
- [ ] GA4/GTM/linking readiness was inspected in read-only mode when visible.
- [ ] Tag readiness or diagnostics were inspected without entering setup flows.
- [ ] Recent conversion activity was reviewed for the requested lookback window.
- [ ] Expected conversion actions were compared to observed actions.

## Safety Confirmation

- [ ] No conversion action or goal was created, edited, removed, or reclassified.
- [ ] No tag, GA4, GTM, enhanced conversion, or offline import setting changed.
- [ ] No linked account was linked, unlinked, accepted, rejected, or edited.
- [ ] No save, apply, publish, continue, or confirm action was clicked.

## Gate A

- [ ] Human reviewed the audit.
- [ ] Human approved with `approve audit` or `audit looks good`, or requested
      corrections.
